Meet Our Staff

Jaelin Slater, Executive Producer

“My name is Jaelin and I am a graduating senior Psychology and Forensic Science major. I joined Live from Studio B as a freshman writer and this show got me into comedy and led to me meeting so many wonderful, talented people and I learned so many writing and production skills that led me to becoming the current Executive Producer! Not only did this show introduce me to sketch comedy and television experience, but I had the opportunity to learn how to perform stand-up comedy and improv!”

Aine Hunt, Head Writer (1/2)

“My name is Aine and I am a head writer at LFSB. This will be my second semester as head writer of the show. LFSB is a community of hilarious, supportive individuals. I’ve met some of my closest friends through this show. Last semester we were gifted with six super funny new writers and now we’re looking forward to hiring even funnier newbies this semester. Watching the new writers grow as comedians has been my favorite part of my position.”

Lucas Portnoy, Head Writer (2/)

“My name is Lucas. I am a junior TRF major and a new head writer alongside Aine. I have been a writer for Studio B since my freshman year and I am very excited to start my new position. I have made some great friends and had a lot of fun being in the writers’ room. I am looking forward to helping to continue to make this show as great as it has been and find ways to keep innovating. I am also looking forward to working with all of our great writers.”

Gretchen Harken, Head of Content Creation

“Hi! My name is Gretchen, and I am a Junior dual majoring in TRF and Information Management & Technology. This is my sixth season with Studio B. This show is filled with some of the most talented and creative students on campus and I am beyond thankful to have had the opportunity to work with all of them for the past 5 seasons. I love my little content creation family and can’t wait to begin a new season!"

Sophia Pappas, Director
